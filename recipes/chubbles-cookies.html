Chubbles Cookies

Fantastic and festive rabbit themed cookies for any occassion!

Ingredients

  • One cup of cubble butter
  • 2 teaspoons of roundness
  • 1 cup of diced carrots

Baking Steps

  1. Place all above listed ingredients in a bowl
  2. Place onto baking sheet and use bunny cutout to size cookies
  3. Bake on high for 15 minutes or until golden brown
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes before eating
  5. Enjoy with your favorite (furry) friends!
